About Us

Serving the North of England from our Harrogate and Leeds branches

About Us

With over 20 years in the business, we offer a comprehensive range of products to suit all budgets. We don’t do “Special Offers” or “Today Only” Prices. The price we quote you will be the price you pay (*).

HOW WE WORK

All our shutters are made to order and bespoke to your requirements. We don’t use high pressure sales methods. We will quote you a price based on your own measurements, which will include delivery, fitting and a 5 year comprehensive warranty. (of course, we will happily come out and take the measurements for you).

We will explain the various options available from our product portfolio (Material, Colours, Louvre Widths, Splits and controls), and we will then put your quote together.

If you’re happy, we will come and take manufacturing measurements, and finalise any of the finer details. Then when your shutters arrive, we will check them and arrange to come and fit them for you. Most jobs can be done in a day.

WHY CHOOSE SHUTTERS?

Choose from the widest range of styles, colours and premium materials, from ABS and MDF to 100% hardwood - there is a range to suit every budget, requirement and taste. Our made-to-measure shutters are produced in the world’s largest shutter factory with state-of-the art facilities. High quality layers of paint or stain are applied, including a final UV layer to protect from fading.

A STYLISH NEW LOOK

Our shutters are more than just a window covering. They are a lifestyle feature, designed to function around your needs, exactly as you’d pictured it.

Any shape fitted perfectly. Our shutters can be manufactured to suit all window shapes, no matter how curved or angled, small or large.

Premium window shutters can genuinely add to the resale value of your property. When viewed from the road, they stand out and make a confident design statement. To any property buyer, it also instantly says, “here’s a home that’s been lovingly looked after”.

ULTIMATE LIGHT CONTROL

Each panel can be independently adjusted to temper the light and provide outstanding light control.

Our shutters are designed so that each panel can be independently adjusted to temper the light exactly as you like it. They give you complete control of your home, so you needn’t risk faded fabrics or need to draw the curtain or move from where you’re sitting because the sun is in the wrong place!

TOTAL PRIVACY WHEN IT SUITS YOU

Each panel can be individually closed to allow light in but keep unwanted attention out. Close the lower panels for privacy, but allow light in from the upper panels. Ideal for bedrooms, bathrooms and street facing windows.

INSULATED AND ECONOMICAL

Our shutters can reduce heat loss through windows by as much as 50%, depending on which style you choose. They’re thermally dynamic, too, so they’ll ‘reflect’ cold from the outside, insulating your home and saving you money at the same time.

LOW MAINTENANCE

If low maintenance is high on your agenda, then you’ll be pleased to know that our shutters make for light work! They’re easy to clean, usually just one wipe, or a quick dusting, and you’re done. Unlike curtains, which can act like giant dust traps, our shutters are great for minimising the effects of dust if you have an allergy.
